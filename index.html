Bitconic is building the future

Bitconic envisions the future of crypto infrastructure, anticipating its trajectory over the next 5 to 10 years. By recognizing the infrastructure of the new financial system can be split into three different technology battles: ‍
  1. The Currency/Settlement Layer battle
  2. The Virtual Machine battle
  3. The Scalability battle
Bitconic determined the victors in these pivotal technological arenas to be a Layer 2 EVM on top of Bitcoin.

The Layer 1 Battle

Bitconic harnesses the power of Bitcoin as the bedrock of true decentralization.Unrivaled in security and trust, Bitcoin's status as the most decentralized asset forties our commitment to building on the ultimate settlement layer

Virtual Machine Battle

Capitalizing on Ethereum's thriving ecosystem and dominant lead in applications and developers, the Ethereum Virtual Machine reigns as the premier virtual machine. Just as Windows prevailed in the '80s, momentum drives the victor!

Scalability Battle

Bitconic embraces alayered strategy,eclipsing bridge solutions with unmatched security. By directly inherting Bitcoin's robust protection, Bitconic fortifies your assets with the pinnacle of crypto defence.

We are building an EVM on top of Bitcoin

Bitconic unites Bitcoin's market cap with Ethereum's dApp ecosystem, fusing the best of both worlds.
Bitcoin's fair distribution, decentralization, and unparalleled security meet Ethereum's diverse applications, igniting a new frontier.
